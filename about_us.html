Meet the Founders

Olivier and Louis, the founders of Rose Poem, spent their formative years in Paris. And, while they like to consider themselves smooth, they accepted early on the fact that even they needed help with their game sometimes so they often turned to roses throughout their young adulthood for multiple occasions.

But then, they moved to New York City, where they soon found out - the hard way - that roses didn't have the same clout they seemed to have in Paris. Why? Because no matter how hard they tried, they couldn't find quality, breathtaking arrangements. They tried the Internet. No luck. Local florists. Nothing. Markets. Nope. Plenty of cheesy arrangements, cheap packaging and high price tags. Not the simplistic beauty of rose bouquets they were accustomed to finding just about anywhere in Paris.

They realized that if they couldn't find what they were looking for, neither could anyone else. So they took action and created Rose Poem. True to its name, the company sells rose bouquets arranged with seasonally appropriate foliage. And, perhaps the best part for anyone trying to make a great impression: you get the WOW factor. At Rose Poem, each rose is selected for its beauty to both the eye and the nose. Each bouquet is lovingly designed in the company's workshops by trained Rose Poem florists who have mastered the French art of bouquet design. Each arrangement is carefully packaged using products that ensure it will arrive - and stay - fresh.

So what do you have to say today? For all occasions and non-occasions, say it with an Rose Poem bouquet.
